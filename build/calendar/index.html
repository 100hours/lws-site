25 October, National Portrait Gallery

St Martin's Place, London WC2H 0HE

David Varela

Part of the NPG Late Shift series, The Elizabethans Undressed is an evening of costume, lectures and performances focusing on the Elizabethan era. David Varela will be the ‘scribe in residence’ from 5-10pm, taking commissions from a modern generation of literary patrons.

1 November, Woolfson & Tay Bookshop

39 Bear Lane, London SE1 0UH

Sarah Butler

Novelist Sarah Butler will spend the day in this quirky independent bookshop and café which offers a dynamic, creative, community space in Bankside.

7 November, London-wide

London

Gemma Seltzer

Gemma Seltzer will be going mobile around London, writing live from a variety of locations in an online-only pop-up residency. Keep an eye out for her.

13 November, The Jewish Museum

129-131 Albert Street, London NW1 7NB

David Baddiel

The Museum houses a display of religious artifacts and a new exhibition on Jewish involvement in football. We’re yet to confirm the writer for this venue but it’s going to be someone great. Watch this space.

20 November, Deptford Lounge

9 Giffin Street, London SE8 4RJ

Jacob Sam-La Rose

Poet Jacob Sam-La Rose will be incorporating live writing into his workshop with the Podium Poets , a group of young writers from London. The event will take place in the multi-award-winning Deptford Lounge building, and is supported by literature development agency Spread the Word.

30 November, The Roundhouse

Chalk Farm Road, London NW1 8EH

Molly Naylor

Molly Naylor will be in residence in this thriving cultural venue as part of the UK’s first spoken word festival, The Last Word, showcasing the talents of emerging and established performers.

4 December, Southbank Centre

Belvedere Road, London SE1 8XX

TBC

As part of the Southbank Centre’s autumn literature programme, the writer (TBC) will exercise their writing talents in the Clore Ballroom of the Royal Festival Hall as well as in other corners of the building.
